Smart Budgeting

"Budgeting made easier"

Description

Our application Smart Budget helps users budget their income while maintaining their savings. The user inputs they annual income, fixed expenses, and a goal of how muuch they want to save.(i.e : John wants to save $3,500 to buy some AirPods and a new Mac).
The user then picks a scenario from different scenarios. Each scenario priortizes a category more than the other (i.e : Alyssa prefers having a large food budget, so she would choose Senario 1. Mark would likely lean more towards scenario three, since he's making his way through college and dislikes student loans.)
After a user picks a scenario, they immediately recieve income divided between seven categories: food, personal expenses/ entertainment, medical expenses, utilities, emergency, education, and transportation. The user's budgets are allocated so they can still reach the amount they want to save.
